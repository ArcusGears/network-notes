Questions on Reading

  1. What is the difference between a DDoS attack and a DoS attack, and why is it harder to protect against?
    • The source of the attack is distributed in a DDoS, so instead of blocking just one source, you must block multiple, which is much harder to do.
  2. What year (give or take 6 years) was the first packet switch installed?
    • 1969
  3. What is the equation for the end-to-end Delay for one packet and what does each constant/variable stand for?
    • a. dend-to-end=N(L/R)
    • b. N is the number of links. L is the number of bits and R is the transmission rate (bits/second)
  4. 3. What are 4 of the 5 layers of a 5 layer internet protocol stack?
    • Application, Transport, Network, Link, Physical
  5. What is the main difference between a client-server architecture and a p2p architecture?
    • A client-server has a server that connects between the clients. In a p2p architecture, the clients connect to each other directly. Those clients are called peers.
  6. What is the main purpose of cookies?
    • To allow sites to keep track of its users
  7. What protocols do HTTP and SMTP use? How are they different?
    • a. HTTP uses a pull protocol and SMTP uses push protocol.
    • b. A push protocol will push the information to the receiver, while a pull protocol will load the information on a web server and the client can pull the information at any time.
  8. Describe the rarest first technique.
    • When torrenting, out of the chunks you don't have, you request to rarest (least amount of uploaders have the chunk) chunk first.
