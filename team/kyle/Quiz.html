Term Definition
Packet Switching A form of data transmission which allocates a specific amount of bandwidth to form a continuous line of communication between end users.
Circuit Switching A form of multiplexing which allocates a specific bandwidth for each connection.
Frequency-Division Multiplexing A form of data transmission which splits up messages into byte sized parts and transmits them piecemeal through nodes in a network.
Time-Division Multiplexing A form of multiplexing which rapidly cycles through each connection using the entire bandwidth.
